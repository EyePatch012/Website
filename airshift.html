Personal replicated multiplayer PvP prototype

Airshift is my first polished replicated multiplayer game: a fast third-person PvP shooter built around networked movement, combat, weapons, stats, and multiplayer AI enemies.

Visual elements are mostly assets, with some made or adjusted by me; my focus was building and polishing the mechanics.

What I Built

  • Replicated player movement, stat systems, combat, and weapon behavior.
  • Built client-server event matching for timing-sensitive actions such as montage playback.
  • Created replicated AI enemies for multiplayer sessions.
  • Implemented advanced player and AI movement, including running, dashing, double jumping, mantling, flying, and dodging.
  • Used the project to apply and polish everything I learned about Unreal replication.
