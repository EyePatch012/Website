Cyber Trap is a synth-heavy 2.5D platformer about outrunning rogue security systems. I developed the traversal mechanics in Unreal with spline-based moving hazards and reactive UI that shows how close you are to getting traced.

  • Wall-runs, grapples, and momentum-based slides for expressive movement.
  • Dynamic soundtrack layers that react to alarm levels.
  • Environmental storytelling told through holographic terminals.
