About Me

I'm Ray, a game developer and Unreal programmer studying Creative Media and Game Technology. I care most about making mechanics feel responsive, readable, and fun: combat that has impact, AI that creates pressure, movement that gives players control, and systems that are clean enough to expand. My work is mostly focused on gameplay programming, Blueprint architecture, replication, and fast prototyping.

Education

Bachelor of Science in Creative Media & Game Technologies at Hanze University of Applied Sciences in the Netherlands. Before moving into game development, I completed two years toward a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering at North South University in Bangladesh, then chose to follow my passion for building games.
