About Me

I’m Ray, a passionate and versatile game developer, digital artist, and enthusiastic gamer. Currently studying Creative Media and Game Technology, I specialize in programming using Unreal Engine 4/5. I’m committed to continuously learning and growing in the game industry, with a focus on making engaging, fun, and visually appealing games.
