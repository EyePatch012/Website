About Me

I’m Ray, a passionate and versatile game developer, digital artist, and enthusiastic gamer. Currently studying Creative Media and Game Technology, I specialize in programming using Unreal Engine 4/5. I enjoy blending fast-paced combat mechanics inspired by titles like Devil May Cry with rich environmental storytelling, and I’m always eager to explore new creative ideas from concept art to character design and game mechanics. I’m committed to continuously learning and growing in the game industry, with a focus on making engaging, fun, and visually appealing games.
