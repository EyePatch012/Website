Neo Pulse is my neon-soaked homage to stylish action combat. I coded the entire experience in Unreal Engine and focused on reactive enemy AI, air-juggle combat, and satisfying visual feedback that pulses to the beat of the soundtrack.

  • Custom combat system with cancel windows, launchers, and aerial finishers.
  • Boss encounters that learn from the player’s favorite combos.
  • Shader-driven VFX that sync with hit impacts and particle trails.
