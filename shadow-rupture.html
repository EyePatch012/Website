Personal combat, movement, and AI showcase

Shadow Rupture is my first large personal Unreal project, inspired by DMC-style hack-and-slash combat. It focuses on fast melee action, expressive movement, and enemy AI tiers that challenge the player in different ways.

Visual elements are mostly assets, with some made or adjusted by me; my focus was on the combat, movement, AI, and gameplay systems.

What I Built

  • Built a fast hack-and-slash melee combat system with a strong focus on game feel.
  • Created multiple AI enemy tiers to support different combat challenges.
  • Implemented movement mechanics including dash, grapple, and double jump.
  • Integrated Unreal's ALS framework as the animation and locomotion foundation.
  • Directed cinematic finishing moves with Sequencer.
